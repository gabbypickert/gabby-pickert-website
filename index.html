About Me

Hello, I'm
Gabby.

Hi! I'm Gabby! I'm from a tiny ski town in Montana, currently living out my theatre kid dreams in New York. I started performing at 4 years old and fell in love with musical theatre by the second grade. I am currently serving as assistant director on a new musical based on the songs of Foreigner, Feels Like the First Time, directed by Adam Pascal. This process has opened my eyes to the exciting world of creating new work, which I hope to continue doing until I'm old and grey. I LOVE theatre and am sure to bring enthusiasm, a great eye for detail, and a darn good batch of chocolate chip cookies to any show I'm working on.

Outside of the rehearsal room, I love to crochet, read, bake, and watch Desperate Housewives. I am also a proud tree hugger and I am obsessed with being in the mountains — skiing, hiking, climbing, and swimming.
