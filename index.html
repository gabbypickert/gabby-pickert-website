About Me

Hello, I'm
Gabby.

🌸

A stage and screen actor based in New York City, I've spent the last decade telling stories that make audiences laugh, cry, and question everything in between.

Trained at the Atlantic Theater Company and the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, I bring a rigorous physical and emotional approach to every role — from classical Shakespeare to contemporary indie film.

Off-stage, you'll find me in my garden, reading scripts under cherry blossoms, or workshopping new plays with the community theater collective I co-founded.
