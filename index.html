About Me

Hello, I'm
Gabby.

A director and performer based in New York, I'm currently pursuing my BFA in Musical Theater at Long Island University (Class of 2026), where I've had the joy of working alongside incredible mentors and collaborators.

My work spans directing, assistant directing, performance, and music direction — from originating roles in new works to leading workshops and showcases. I'm passionate about telling stories that move people and creating spaces where artists can do their best work.

Most recently I've been working on Tick Tick Boom, Feels Like the First Time directed by Adam Pascal, and have performed as Cinderella in Into the Woods and Maureen in Rent.
